# Sample Wizard Session: Two-Phase Complete Walkthrough

This example shows a complete brand guidelines onboarding session for "TechFlow Analytics" - a fictional B2B SaaS company, including both Phase 1 and Phase 2.

**Total Time**: ~16 minutes
- Phase 1 (Core Branding): ~8 minutes
- Choice Point: ~1 minute
- Phase 2 (Enhancement): ~7 minutes

---

## User Initiation

**User**: "Set up brand guidelines for my company"

**Claude**: "Let's set up your brand guidelines! Do you have existing brand documentation?"

Options:
1. Paste brand document text (Recommended for fastest setup)
2. Provide file path or URL to brand document
3. Start from scratch with guided questions

**User**: "Start from scratch"

**Claude**: "Perfect! I'll guide you through creating comprehensive brand guidelines step-by-step. This will take about 15 minutes."

---

## Brand Foundation

**Claude**: "What's your company or brand name?"

**User**: "TechFlow Analytics"

**Claude**: "What's your brand's purpose or mission? (1-2 sentences)"

**User**: "We empower small businesses with enterprise-grade analytics tools they can actually afford and understand."

**Claude**: "What 3-5 values define your brand?"

**User**: "Accessibility, Transparency, Innovation, Customer-First"

---

## Brand Voice

**Claude**: "Describe your brand voice in 2-4 words. This should stay constant across ALL content."

**User**: "Approachable, Insightful, Trustworthy"

**Claude**: "Great! Now let's define what each attribute means for your brand."

**Claude**: "What does 'Approachable' mean for your brand?"

**User**: "We make complex analytics simple. We explain things clearly without jargon, like a knowledgeable friend helping you understand data."

Example: "Your customers visited 3x more this month. Let's see why."

**Claude**: "What does 'Insightful' mean for your brand?"

**User**: "We don't just show data - we explain what it means and what to do about it. We provide context and actionable recommendations."

Example: "Weekend traffic is up 40%. Consider extending your weekend support hours."

**Claude**: "What does 'Trustworthy' mean for your brand?"

**User**: "We're honest about what our product can and can't do. We never over-promise. We protect customer data with enterprise-grade security."

Example: "We use bank-level encryption. Your data never leaves our secure servers."

---

## Brand Tone

**Claude**: "What contexts do you create content for? (Select all that apply)"

**User**: "Product Specs, Social Media, Support, Marketing"

**Claude**: "What tone should you use for Product Specs?"

**User**: "Clear & Technical - precise feature descriptions without marketing fluff. Example: 'Real-time dashboard updates. 99.9% uptime SLA. GDPR compliant.'"

**Claude**: "What tone should you use for Social Media?"

**User**: "Helpful & Engaging - share tips and insights, encourage discussion. Example: 'Quick tip: Filter your dashboard by date range to spot seasonal trends 📊'"

**Claude**: "What tone should you use for Support?"

**User**: "Patient & Solution-Oriented - empathetic, step-by-step help. Example: 'I see the issue. Let's fix that together. First, click Settings in the top right...'"

**Claude**: "What tone should you use for Marketing?"

**User**: "Value-Focused - emphasize benefits and ROI, not features. Example: 'Stop guessing. Start knowing. See what drives your business growth.'"

## Generated File Preview

```markdown
# Brand Guidelines: TechFlow Analytics

## 1. Brand Mission & Philosophy

We empower small businesses with enterprise-grade analytics tools they can actually afford and understand.

**Core Values**:
- **Accessibility**: Making powerful analytics available to everyone
- **Transparency**: Clear pricing, honest capabilities, open communication
- **Innovation**: Constantly improving based on customer feedback
- **Customer-First**: Every decision prioritizes customer success

## 2. Brand Voice (Constant)

### Approachable
- **What it means**: We make complex analytics simple
- **Language style**: Clear explanations without jargon, like a helpful friend
- **Example**: "Your customers visited 3x more this month. Let's see why."
- **Not this**: "Implement advanced cohort segmentation algorithms"

### Insightful
- **What it means**: We explain what data means and what to do about it
- **Language style**: Context and actionable recommendations, not just numbers
- **Example**: "Weekend traffic is up 40%. Consider extending your weekend support hours."
- **Not this**: "Weekend sessions: 2,340"

### Trustworthy
- **What it means**: Honest about capabilities, enterprise-grade security
- **Language style**: Never over-promise, transparent about limitations
- **Example**: "We use bank-level encryption. Your data never leaves our secure servers."
- **Not this**: "We're the best analytics platform ever!"

[... rest of sections ...]
```
