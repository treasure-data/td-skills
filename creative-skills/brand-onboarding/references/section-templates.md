## Formatting Guidelines

### Markdown Conventions

**Headings**:
- `#` for main title
- `##` for major sections (1-8)
- `###` for subsections (A, B, C, etc.)
- `####` for individual items

**Lists**:
- Use `-` for unordered lists
- Use `**Bold**:` for list item labels
- Use numbered lists only for sequential steps

**Code blocks**:
- Use triple backticks for templates and footers
- Use inline code (backticks) for hex codes, CSS, file paths

**Emphasis**:
- `**Bold**` for labels, color names, section names
- `*Italic*` sparingly for emphasis
- `"{Quotes}"` for example content

**Symbols**:
- ✅ for approved/recommended
- ❌ for prohibited/avoid
- ⚠️ for warnings

### File Organization

**Order of sections** (always):
1. Brand Mission & Philosophy
2. Brand Voice (Constant)
3. Brand Tone (Variable by Context)
4. Visual Identity (Colors, Typography, Logo, Imagery)
5. Messaging & Lexicon
6. Legal & Compliance Requirements
7. Accessibility Standards
8. Compliance Checklist

**Optional sections** can be omitted:
- Logo files (if not provided)
- Specific legal sections (if channel not used)
- Imagery guidelines (if minimal)

**Required minimum**:
- Sections 1-5 and 8 are always required
- Section 6 requires at least one channel
- Section 7 is strongly recommended
