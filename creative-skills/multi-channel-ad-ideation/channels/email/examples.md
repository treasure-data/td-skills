## Example 3: B2B Thought Leadership

**Concept: Value-First Content**

**Creative Direction**: Following the "Benefit-Focused" approach - Lead with free value, position as expert, soft-sell at end.

### Ad Copy

- **Subject Line**: "The 5 pipeline mistakes costing you deals [free guide]" (56 chars)
- **Preview Text**: "Plus: how to fix them in under 30 days" (38 chars)
- **Headline**: Are These Pipeline Mistakes Costing You Deals?
- **Body Copy**:
  After analyzing 10,000+ sales pipelines, we found 5 mistakes that appear in 87% of underperforming teams.

  The good news? All 5 are fixable in under 30 days.

  Download our free guide to learn:
  • Why "gut feel" forecasting fails (and what works instead)
  • The #1 reason deals stall in your pipeline
  • How to identify at-risk deals before it's too late
  • The 3 metrics top performers track daily
  • Simple process changes that boost close rates 15-30%

  This isn't theory - it's based on real data from 500+ sales teams.

  Grab your free copy and start closing more deals next month.
- **CTA Button**: Download Free Guide

### Visual Concept (Description Only)

- **Layout**: Text-heavy with clean formatting, minimal graphics
- **Color Palette**: Professional blue (#2c5f8d) for headline and CTA, white background, dark gray (#333) text
- **Imagery**: Simple icon graphic of upward trending chart or abstract data visualization (no stock photos)
- **Typography**: Serif font for headline to convey authority (28px), sans-serif for body text (16px), clean bullet formatting
