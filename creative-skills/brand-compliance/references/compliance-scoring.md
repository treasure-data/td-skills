# Brand Compliance Scoring Rubric

Use this rubric to assess brand guideline compliance across creative content. The scoring system helps identify violations and guide fixes to align with brand standards.

## Overview

**Total Possible Score**: 40 points (8 dimensions × 5 points each)

**Compliance Tiers**:
- **38-40**: Fully Compliant ✅ - Ready to launch
- **32-37**: Mostly Compliant ⚠️ - Minor fixes needed
- **24-31**: Partially Compliant ⚠️ - Significant issues to address
- **0-23**: Non-Compliant ❌ - Major revision required

---

## 8 Compliance Dimensions

### 1. Visual Compliance (0-5 points)

**What it measures**: Adherence to brand visual identity (colors, typography, logos, imagery)

**Scoring**:
- **5**: Perfect visual compliance - all colors, fonts, logos match brand guidelines exactly
- **4**: Strong compliance - minor deviations (e.g., slight color shade difference)
- **3**: Moderate compliance - some visual elements off-brand (wrong font weight, missing logo)
- **2**: Weak compliance - multiple visual violations (wrong colors, wrong fonts)
- **1**: Minimal compliance - major visual violations across multiple elements
- **0**: No compliance - completely off-brand visuals

**Examples**:
- ✅ **Score 5**: Uses brand colors #1B3022, #B35D33, #F5F2ED; Montserrat headings, Roboto body; logo properly sized
- ⚠️ **Score 3**: Uses Arial instead of Roboto (font violation), but colors correct
- ❌ **Score 1**: Red CTA (#FF0000) instead of Burnt Ochre (#B35D33), Comic Sans font, no logo

---

### 2. Tone/Voice Compliance (0-5 points)

**What it measures**: Adherence to brand voice attributes and contextual tone

**Scoring**:
- **5**: Perfect tone/voice - matches all brand voice attributes and contextual tone guidelines
- **4**: Strong compliance - voice attributes present, minor tone contextual mismatch
- **3**: Moderate compliance - some voice attributes missing or tone inappropriate for context
- **2**: Weak compliance - voice doesn't match brand personality
- **1**: Minimal compliance - tone completely wrong for context
- **0**: No compliance - opposite of brand voice

**Examples (Acme Corp)**:
- ✅ **Score 5**: Product specs use "Engineered with bluesign® nylon" (Knowledgeable + Precise/Functional tone)
- ⚠️ **Score 3**: Product specs say "What's your sunrise ritual?" (wrong context - too inspirational for specs)
- ❌ **Score 1**: Uses casual tone "This is lit!" instead of Knowledgeable/Enthusiastic

---

### 3. Messaging Compliance (0-5 points)

**What it measures**: Use of approved terminology, avoidance of prohibited terms, key messages present

**Scoring**:
- **5**: Perfect messaging - uses all approved terms, avoids all prohibited terms, key messages present
- **4**: Strong compliance - mostly approved terminology, one minor prohibited term
- **3**: Moderate compliance - mix of approved/prohibited terms
- **2**: Weak compliance - multiple prohibited terms, missing key messages
- **1**: Minimal compliance - predominantly prohibited terminology
- **0**: No compliance - all prohibited terms, no approved messaging

**Examples (Acme Corp)**:
- ✅ **Score 5**: "Circular materials", "PFC-free", "Traceable supply chain" (all approved)
- ⚠️ **Score 3**: "Eco-friendly" instead of "circular" (prohibited term), but uses "PFC-free" correctly
- ❌ **Score 1**: "Green", "eco-friendly", "life-changing", "insane" (all prohibited terms)

---

### 4. Legal Compliance (0-5 points)

**What it measures**: Required disclaimers, regulatory compliance, substantiated claims

**Scoring**:
- **5**: Perfect legal compliance - all disclaimers present, claims substantiated, regulations met
- **4**: Strong compliance - minor missing element (e.g., footer placement suboptimal)
- **3**: Moderate compliance - missing one required disclaimer
- **2**: Weak compliance - multiple legal violations
- **1**: Minimal compliance - major regulatory violations
- **0**: No compliance - no legal requirements met

**Channel-specific requirements**:
- **Email**: CAN-SPAM footer (unsubscribe + physical address)
- **SMS**: TCPA opt-out ("Text STOP to unsubscribe")
- **Instagram**: FTC disclosure (#ad, #sponsored if paid promotion)

**Examples**:
- ✅ **Score 5**: Email has unsubscribe link, physical address, all claims cited
- ⚠️ **Score 3**: Email missing physical address in footer
- ❌ **Score 1**: SMS with no opt-out instructions, unsubstantiated sustainability claims

---

### 5. Channel Requirements (0-5 points)

**What it measures**: Channel-specific best practices and platform requirements

**Scoring**:
- **5**: Perfect channel fit - follows all channel best practices and requirements
- **4**: Strong fit - minor channel optimization missed
- **3**: Acceptable fit - some channel requirements not met
- **2**: Poor fit - multiple channel violations
- **1**: Very poor fit - doesn't follow channel basics
- **0**: Doesn't work - wrong format for channel

**Channel-specific checks**:
- **Email**: Subject 40-60 chars, preview text 35-55 chars, mobile-responsive, single CTA
- **SMS**: 160 chars (single) or clearly multi-part, plain text only, opt-out included
- **Instagram**: Primary text ≤125 chars, headline ≤40 chars, description ≤30 chars, approved CTA button

**Examples**:
- ✅ **Score 5**: Email subject 52 chars, preview 45 chars, responsive HTML, clear CTA
- ⚠️ **Score 3**: Instagram primary text 150 chars (cut off after 125)
- ❌ **Score 1**: SMS at 300 chars without multi-part indicator, HTML formatting attempted

---

### 6. Accessibility (0-5 points)

**What it measures**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, inclusive design

**Scoring**:
- **5**: Fully accessible - meets all WCAG AA requirements (4.5:1 contrast, alt text, font sizes)
- **4**: Mostly accessible - minor accessibility issue (e.g., one image missing alt text)
- **3**: Partially accessible - some contrast or text size issues
- **2**: Limited accessibility - multiple violations
- **1**: Poor accessibility - major barriers present
- **0**: Inaccessible - fails basic accessibility

**Requirements**:
- Color contrast: Minimum 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text (WCAG AA)
- Font sizes: Minimum 16px for mobile body text
- Alt text: Descriptive text for all images
- Screen reader: Semantic HTML, proper heading hierarchy

**Examples**:
- ✅ **Score 5**: All text 4.5:1+ contrast, 16px+ fonts, alt text on all images
- ⚠️ **Score 3**: Light gray text on white background (2.8:1 contrast - fails)
- ❌ **Score 1**: 12px font, no alt text, decorative images as content

---

### 7. Brand Identity Strength (0-5 points)

**What it measures**: How recognizable and distinct the brand presence is

**Scoring**:
- **5**: Unmistakably brand - immediately recognizable, strong brand presence
- **4**: Clearly brand - recognizable with brand elements prominent
- **3**: Moderately brand - some brand elements present but generic
- **2**: Weakly brand - barely recognizable as brand
- **1**: Generic - could be any brand
- **0**: Anonymous - no brand identity visible

**Examples**:
- ✅ **Score 5**: Logo, brand colors, signature "Gear up. Get out. Leave it better.", unique visual style
- ⚠️ **Score 3**: Uses brand colors but generic layout, no unique elements
- ❌ **Score 1**: No logo, generic fonts, could be any outdoor company

---

### 8. Guideline Adherence (0-5 points)

**What it measures**: Overall adherence to all documented brand guidelines

**Scoring**:
- **5**: Perfect adherence - follows all documented guidelines
- **4**: Strong adherence - follows nearly all guidelines, one minor deviation
- **3**: Moderate adherence - follows most guidelines, some violations
- **2**: Weak adherence - multiple guideline violations
- **1**: Minimal adherence - ignores most guidelines
- **0**: No adherence - disregards all guidelines

**Examples**:
- ✅ **Score 5**: Follows visual, messaging, tone, legal, accessibility guidelines
- ⚠️ **Score 3**: Violates lexicon (uses "eco-friendly"), wrong contextual tone, but visuals correct
- ❌ **Score 1**: Multiple violations across visual, messaging, tone, legal dimensions

### Dimension Bar Colors

**Individual dimension scoring** (bar colors by score):
- 5 points: Green `#10b981` (perfect)
- 4 points: Amber `#f59e0b` (strong)
- 3 points: Blue `#3b82f6` (acceptable)
- 2 points: Orange `#fb923c` (needs improvement)
- 1 point: Red `#ef4444` (weak)
- 0 points: Gray `#9ca3af` (missing)

**Bar width calculation**:
- Width = (score / 5) × 100%
- Example: 4/5 = 80% width

## Sample Scored Content

### Email: Acme Corp Hiking Boots Launch

**Content**: Email promoting new circular-material hiking boots

**Violations**:
- Messaging: Uses "eco-friendly" (prohibited term)
- Visual: CTA button is red (#FF0000) instead of Burnt Ochre (#B35D33)
- Legal: Missing physical address in footer

### Scoring:

1. **Visual Compliance**: 3/5 - Wrong CTA color, but fonts and logo correct
2. **Tone/Voice Compliance**: 5/5 - Knowledgeable and enthusiastic tone maintained
3. **Messaging Compliance**: 3/5 - Uses prohibited "eco-friendly", but other terms approved
4. **Legal Compliance**: 3/5 - Has unsubscribe link but missing physical address
5. **Channel Requirements**: 5/5 - Subject 48 chars, preview 42 chars, mobile-responsive
6. **Accessibility**: 5/5 - All text 4.5:1+ contrast, 16px fonts, alt text present
7. **Brand Identity Strength**: 4/5 - Logo and colors present, but CTA color off-brand
8. **Guideline Adherence**: 3/5 - Multiple violations (terminology, visual, legal)

**Total Score: 31/40 (Mostly Compliant)**

### Recommended Fixes:
1. **Messaging**: Replace "eco-friendly" → "circular-material" or "PFC-free"
2. **Visual**: Change CTA color from #FF0000 → #B35D33 (Burnt Ochre)
3. **Legal**: Add company physical address to footer

**After Fixes**: Score would increase to 39/40 (Fully Compliant)

## Using Scores to Guide Fixes

### Fully Compliant (38-40)
**Action**: Ready to launch. Optional minor refinements only.

**Example feedback**:
> "Excellent! Scored 39/40 - Fully Compliant. All brand guidelines met. One optional enhancement: add brand signature 'Gear up. Get out. Leave it better.' for stronger brand presence."

### Mostly Compliant (32-37)
**Action**: Fix 1-2 specific violations before launch.

**Example feedback**:
> "Mostly Compliant (34/40). Two fixes needed: 1) Replace 'eco-friendly' with 'circular', 2) Add physical address to footer. These changes will bring you to 40/40."

### Partially Compliant (24-31)
**Action**: Address multiple violations across several dimensions.

**Example feedback**:
> "Partially Compliant (28/40). Multiple issues to address: Wrong CTA color (#FF0000 → #B35D33), prohibited terms ('eco-friendly' → 'circular'), missing footer address, tone too casual for product specs context. Fix these to reach compliance."

### Non-Compliant (0-23)
**Action**: Major revision required. Consider starting fresh with brand guidelines.

**Example feedback**:
> "Non-Compliant (18/40). Major brand violations: Uses all prohibited terms (eco-friendly, green, insane, epic), wrong fonts (Arial instead of Roboto), no legal disclaimers, and tone doesn't match brand voice. Recommend reviewing brand guidelines and regenerating content."

---

## Summary

The brand compliance rubric helps you:
- **Objectively assess** content against brand guidelines
- **Identify violations** across visual, messaging, tone, legal, and accessibility dimensions
- **Prioritize fixes** by severity and impact
- **Track compliance** across campaigns and channels
- **Maintain brand consistency** at scale

Use this rubric after generating content or when reviewing existing materials to ensure all creative aligns with brand standards.
