# Slide Layout Patterns

## Template Structure

- **Dimensions**: Widescreen 13.33" x 7.50" (standard 16:9)
- **Slides in template**: 60
- **Slide layouts**: `5_Title Only`, `Title_6cols`, `1_Title_6cols`

## Common Slide Patterns

### Title Slide
- Deep blue (`#2D40AA`) or dark (`#00042B`) background
- Large heading: Poppins SemiBold, 48pt, white
- Subtitle: Manrope, 16-20pt, `#A5A5A5` or `#7F7F7F`
- Logo placement: bottom-right corner
- Minimal text â€” one strong statement

### Section Divider
- Accent color background (choose from palette accents)
- Section title: Poppins SemiBold, 32-48pt, white or `#000000`
- Optional section number or icon

### Content Slide (Single Column)
- White or `#F7F5F9` background
- Title: Poppins SemiBold, 32pt, `#000000` or `#2D40AA`
- Body: Manrope, 14-16pt, `#000000`
- Muted annotations: Manrope Medium, 9-11pt, `#7F7F7F`

### Two-Column / Comparison
- White background
- Title at top
- Left/right split with content
- Use accent colors to differentiate columns

### Data / Chart Slide
- Title at top: Poppins SemiBold, 32pt
- Chart centered, using brand chart color order
- Source/footnote: Manrope Medium, 9pt, `#A5A5A5`

### Quote / Callout
- Light background (`#F7F5F9`)
- Large quote text: Manrope SemiBold, 24pt
- Attribution: Manrope Medium, 12pt, `#7F7F7F`

### Closing / CTA Slide
- Dark background (`#2D40AA` or `#00042B`)
- Call to action: Poppins SemiBold, 32-48pt, white
- Contact info or next steps: Manrope, 14pt, `#A5A5A5`
- Logo: bottom-right

## Presentation Type Guidelines

### Client Pitch
- Open with bold title slide (brand statement)
- Problem -> Solution -> Proof Points -> CTA
- Use charts and data slides for credibility
- 10-15 slides typical

### Internal Update
- Title slide with date/team name
- Key metrics (data slides)
- Progress highlights (content slides)
- Next steps / blockers
- 5-10 slides typical

### Quarterly Review
- Title slide with quarter
- Executive summary slide
- Metrics/KPI slides (charts)
- Highlights and lowlights
- Forward-looking priorities
- 10-20 slides typical

### Product Demo
- Title slide with product name
- Agenda/overview
- Feature walkthroughs (screenshots + annotations)
- Demo flow (step-by-step)
- Q&A / closing CTA
- 8-15 slides typical
